The most delicious fried rice recipes

How to make the most delicious fried rice recipes you've ever made:

Made with only a handful of wonderful ingredients, this recipes should be simple yet mouth-watering for you and your family.

The ingredients you will need:

  • Cold dried rice (100oz)
  • 2 eggs
  • Soy Sauce (50oz)
  • Garlics and Shallots (10oz)
  • Vegetable of any kind: Carrots, Peas, Corns, (50oz)

Follow these steps:

  1. Stir the garlics and shallots in high heat oil for 1 minute.
  2. Stir-fried the vegetables.
  3. Break in the eggs and combine.
  4. Toss in the rice and stir for 10 minutes.
  5. Season with soy sauce and cook for another 2 minutes before served.
